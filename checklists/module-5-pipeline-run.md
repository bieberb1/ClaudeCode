## Stage 3: Narrate

*Goal: Choose the narrative path. Same Ground Truth, different Story Spines for different audiences.*

**Narration checklist**
- [ ] Target audience defined before writing
- [ ] Value frame matched to what this audience already cares about
- [ ] Emotional register calibrated (not defaulting to the source material's tone)
- [ ] Every fact in the Story Spine is present in the Ground Truth Document
- [ ] No AI-generated statistics used without tracing to the Ground Truth
- [ ] Causal chain ("Because of that") is logically sound
- [ ] Resolution is credible and audience-appropriate

- [ ] Visualizations accurately represent the underlying data
- [ ] AI-generated visuals and text reviewed for accuracy against the Ground Truth
- [ ] Emotional tone of finished piece matches Stage 3 calibration
